## [1.0.0] - 2026-05-15

### Added

- **Hotel Price Comparison** — Compare prices between Agoda and Booking.com simultaneously
- **Agoda Integration** — Playwright-based web scraping for Agoda hotel search and results
- **Booking.com Integration** — Axios + Cheerio-based scraping for Booking.com hotel search and results
- **Arabic-First Interface** — Full RTL layout with Cairo font, Arabic labels, and Hijri-compatible calendar
- **Custom Calendar** — Arabic month names, Hijri weekday abbreviations, month/year selectors
- **Room & Guest Selector** — Dynamic room, adult, and children count controls
- **Express.js 5 Backend** — MVC architecture with controllers, routes, services, and middlewares
- **EJS Templating** — Server-side rendering with Arabic-optimized views
- **Error Handling** — Custom 404 and 500 error pages in Arabic
- **Responsive Design** — Mobile-first CSS with RTL support
- **RED SHADOWS | RS Branding** — Footer with RS logo and link
